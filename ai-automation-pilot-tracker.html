    Measurement Workflow

    Turn pilot activity into reviewable evidence

    The tracker separates observations from interpretation and keeps operational targets distinct from claims about statistical validity or deployment approval.

    1

    Define

    Freeze the bounded workflow, baseline, denominators, costs, planned period, and thresholds.

    2

    Observe

    Record every included case with the same outcome and failure definitions.

    3

    Review

    Inspect corrections, residual errors, exceptions, fallback, failures, incidents, and reviewer notes.

    4

    Decide

    Export the evidence and use the Scorecard for a transparent Scale, Improve, Pause, or Stop review.

    NIST describes measurement as a traceable basis for management decisions and emphasizes documented testing, evaluation, validation, and verification processes. Choose metrics and methods appropriate to the actual context. See the NIST AI Metrology Center and AI RMF Measure Playbook.
