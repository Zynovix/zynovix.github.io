The Five Pillars

What the assessment measures

A strong AI program needs more than tools. These five dimensions work together to turn experiments into measurable, responsible outcomes.

🎯

Strategy

Clear business goals, prioritized use cases, accountable owners, and measurable value.

👥

People

Practical skills, adoption support, workflow ownership, and effective human oversight.

🗂️

Data

Accessible, reliable, classified data plus feedback needed to evaluate AI quality.

⚙️

Technology

Approved tools, secure integrations, access controls, monitoring, and maintainable systems.

🛡️

Governance

Policies, risk reviews, vendor checks, quality controls, and a clear incident process.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

AI readiness is an organization's ability to adopt AI safely and productively. It combines strategy, people, data, technology, and governance—not just access to AI tools.
There are 15 questions, with three questions in each dimension. Every answer scores from 0 to 4. The final score is converted to a percentage, and each dimension receives its own percentage.
Most people finish it in about five minutes. You can move backward to review or change any answer before viewing your result.
No answers are sent to a server. The tool runs entirely in your browser and saves only the latest result locally on your device so you can compare it later.
It is designed for founders, managers, consultants, operations leaders, technology teams, and anyone responsible for planning or improving AI adoption.
