How to estimate automation ROI realistically

A credible estimate includes implementation friction, ongoing costs, and the value of the time genuinely returned to useful work.

⏱️

Measure the current process

Sample real task times across several people and include rework, handoffs, waiting, and exception handling.

⚙️

Reduce theoretical savings

Use coverage and adoption to account for human review, failures, edge cases, and teams that do not use the workflow consistently.

📈

Validate after launch

Compare the estimate with actual usage, error rates, hours saved, maintenance, and whether recovered time creates real value.

Hours saved per month = people × tasks per week × minutes per task ÷ 60 × 4.33 × automation coverage × adoption.
First-year ROI = first-year net benefit ÷ setup, software, and maintenance investment × 100.
