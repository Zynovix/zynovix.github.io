Commercial Use and Copyright

Commercial rights depend on the platform, plan, model license, and generated content. A subscription does not automatically grant exclusive copyright or permission to reproduce protected brands, characters, artists' work, or real people's likenesses.

  • Review the current terms for your exact plan and model.
  • Proofread generated text and inspect small details.
  • Keep records of prompts, references, model versions, and human edits.
  • Avoid protected trademarks and characters without permission.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best AI image generator overall in 2026?

ChatGPT Images 2.0 is the best general recommendation for conversational creation and editing. Midjourney is stronger for artistic style, Ideogram for typography, and Recraft for vectors.

Which AI image generator is best for text?

Ideogram 4.0 is one of the strongest specialist options for readable text in posters, ads, thumbnails, and branded graphics.

Which tool is best for logos and vectors?

Recraft is the strongest recommendation because it can generate scalable vectors, icons, logo concepts, and production-ready design assets.

Which is best for beginners?

ChatGPT and Canva are the easiest starting points because both turn prompts into useful visuals without a complicated technical setup.

Which is best for professional designers?

Adobe Firefly is ideal for Adobe users, while Recraft is excellent for vectors and brand assets. Ideogram is particularly useful for typography.

Which is best for developers?

FLUX, OpenAI's image API, Ideogram, Leonardo, Stable Diffusion, and Google Cloud image models are all strong developer options.

Can AI-generated images be used commercially?

Commercial rights depend on the platform, plan, model license, and content. Review current terms before publishing or selling the output.

Can AI tools keep the same character?

Some tools support image references, custom models, or character-consistency features, but reliable consistency still requires testing and editing.

Is Stable Diffusion still worth using?

Yes. It remains highly relevant for self-hosting, privacy, fine-tuning, LoRA workflows, and custom applications.

Is Midjourney better than ChatGPT Images?

Midjourney often leads for artistic direction, while ChatGPT Images is easier for text, conversational editing, and practical iteration.

How should I compare image generators?

Use identical prompts and compare prompt accuracy, text rendering, editing, consistency, commercial terms, speed, resolution, and finishing effort.

Which tool is best for realistic photos?

ChatGPT Images 2.0, Midjourney V8.1, Recraft V4, Leonardo realism models, and current FLUX models can all produce strong photorealism.

Final Verdict

ChatGPT Images 2.0 is the best general recommendation. Midjourney V8.1 leads for artistic style, Ideogram 4.0 for typography, and Recraft V4 for vectors and practical design assets.

Practical recommendation: Test the same three real prompts in two or three tools and choose the platform that creates the best finished asset with the least editing.
