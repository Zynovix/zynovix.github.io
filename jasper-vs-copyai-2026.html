What is Jasper AI?

Jasper AI (formerly Jarvis) is one of the oldest and most established AI writing tools on the market. Launched in 2021, it's built on top of GPT-4 and is specifically designed for long-form content creation.

Jasper's standout feature is its Brand Voice technology, which learns your company's tone and style, ensuring every piece of content sounds like you — not like a robot.

Key Features:

  • Long-form editor with 3,000+ word capacity
  • Brand Voice customization
  • 50+ templates for blogs, ads, emails
  • Integration with SurferSEO for content optimization
  • Team collaboration tools

What is Copy.ai?

Copy.ai is a newer player (launched in 2020) that focuses on speed and variety. It's built for marketers who need to produce large volumes of short-form content quickly — social media posts, ad copy, email subject lines, and more.

Copy.ai's strength is its 90+ templates and lightning-fast output. It's not designed for writing 3,000-word blog posts — it's designed for churning out 30 Instagram captions in 15 minutes.

Head-to-Head Comparison

1. Content Quality

Winner: Jasper AI

I tested both tools with the exact same prompt: "Write a 1,500-word blog post about the benefits of remote work for small businesses."

Jasper AI result:

  • Well-structured with clear headings
  • Natural-sounding language
  • Only needed minor edits (about 15% of content)
  • Good use of examples and data points
  • Time to publish: 20 minutes (mostly formatting)

Copy.ai result:

  • Decent structure but repetitive
  • More generic language
  • Needed significant editing (about 40% of content)
  • Felt more "AI-generated"
  • Time to publish: 45 minutes

Verdict: If you're writing long-form content, Jasper is clearly better. The quality difference is noticeable.

2. Speed

Winner: Copy.ai

I tested both tools with the same task: "Generate 20 Instagram captions for a fitness brand."

Jasper AI: 40 minutes
Copy.ai: 15 minutes

Copy.ai is significantly faster, especially for short-form content. If speed is your priority, Copy.ai wins.

3. Templates

Winner: Copy.ai

Copy.ai offers 90+ templates compared to Jasper's 50+. More importantly, Copy.ai's templates are more specific and actionable.

For example, Copy.ai has templates for:

  • LinkedIn post hooks
  • YouTube video descriptions
  • Product bullet points
  • Cold email sequences
  • Press releases

Jasper's templates are more general (blog post, ad copy, email) but they work well for what they cover.

4. Brand Voice

Winner: Jasper AI

This is where Jasper really shines. You can train Jasper on your brand's tone, style, and vocabulary. After uploading 3-5 sample documents, Jasper learns to write like your brand.

I tested this with a client's brand guidelines. Jasper's output matched their tone 90% of the time. Copy.ai's Brand Voice feature exists but is less sophisticated — it matched about 70% of the time.

If you're writing for a brand (your own or a client's), Jasper's Brand Voice is a game-changer.
