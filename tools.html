Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our AI tools directory.

How do I choose the right AI tool? +
Start by identifying your specific use case (writing, coding, design, etc.), then consider your budget and skill level. Our interactive quiz above can help you find the perfect match in less than 2 minutes.
Are these AI tools really free? +
We list both free and paid AI tools. Each tool clearly shows its pricing model: "Free" means completely free, "Freemium" means free with optional paid upgrades, and "Paid" means subscription required.
How often do you update reviews? +
We update our reviews monthly to ensure accuracy. AI tools evolve rapidly, so we regularly test new features, pricing changes, and performance improvements.
Can I suggest an AI tool? +
Absolutely! Send us your suggestions through our contact page with the tool name and website. We review all submissions within 48 hours.
Do you receive commissions from these tools? +
Some links may be affiliate links, which means we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you. However, this never influences our reviews or ratings. We maintain complete editorial independence.
